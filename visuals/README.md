# Visual Assets

## Visualizing Organizational Coherence

This section contains visual representations derived from the core structural model:

→ [Organizational Coherence](/aedron-ops-design/conceptual-frameworks/organizational-coherence.html)

These assets translate conceptual frameworks and architectures into **interpretable system views**.

---

Visual assets in this lab are not illustrative diagrams.

They are **structural representations** of:

- decision systems  
- operating architectures  
- KPI and governance layers  
- cross-domain interactions  

---

## Role of Visual Assets

Visuals serve as:

- **interpretation layers** for complex systems  
- **alignment tools** across technical and business stakeholders  
- **navigation maps** for multi-layer architectures  
- **communication bridges** between abstraction and implementation  

They make coherence **visible, traceable, and discussable**.
