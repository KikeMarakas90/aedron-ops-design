# Conceptual Frameworks

## Foundational Layer

This section defines the conceptual foundation of the lab.

All models, blueprints, and patterns are derived from a single structural premise:

→ [Organizational Coherence](organizational-coherence/)

---

Conceptual frameworks in this lab are not abstract theories.

They are **formal structures** that define how:

- decisions remain consistent across domains  
- KPIs align with execution  
- governance enforces coherence  
- intelligence integrates into operating systems  

---

## Role of Conceptual Frameworks

Frameworks operate as:

- the **logic layer** behind all architectures  
- the **structure that prevents contradiction**  
- the **bridge between strategy, metrics, and execution**  

They define how systems remain **coherent under complexity**.
