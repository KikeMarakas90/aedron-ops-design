# Design Patterns

## Mechanisms for Preserving Coherence

This section contains reusable patterns derived from a single structural model:

→ [Organizational Coherence](/aedron-ops-design/conceptual-frameworks/organizational-coherence.html)

Each pattern defines how systems maintain **consistent decision logic under complexity**.

---

Reusable operational and analytical patterns engineered for stability, productivity, visibility, governance, and decision intelligence.

These are not generic best practices.

They are **structural mechanisms that prevent fragmentation in decision systems**.

---

## Pattern Library

- **Operational Stability Pattern (CV, CVM, IQR)**  
  Statistical foundation to govern volatility, outliers, and execution predictability.  

- **Productivity Normalization Pattern**  
  Harmonizing workload, throughput, and performance across heterogeneous teams and geographies.  

- **Semantic KPI Pattern**  
  Standardized structure for multi-domain metrics with governance, lineage, semantic definitions, and ownership.  

- **AI-Assisted Workflow Pattern**  
  Human + machine execution model using LLM/RAG assistants embedded into operational flows.  

---

## Structural Role of Patterns

Patterns exist to address specific failure modes:

- instability under volatility  
- misaligned KPIs across domains  
- inconsistent decision criteria  
- breakdowns between data and execution  

Each pattern provides a **repeatable mechanism to preserve coherence**.

---

## Design Characteristics

Each pattern is:

- **Modular** — reusable across industries and business units  
- **Governed** — aligned to semantic control and decision rights  
- **AI-Native** — extensible to autonomous and augmented systems  
- **Cross-domain** — applicable across complex enterprise environments  
- **Execution-ready** — designed for real-world operational use
